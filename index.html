About ICARE INITIATIVE

Organisation’s Constitution

Name: The name of the organisation shall be the “ICARE INITIATIVE FOR THE SUPPORT AND EMPOWERMENT OF WOMEN”.

Purpose: The organisation creates an assembly of women from diverse backgrounds across Nigeria including professionals, entrepreneurs, community leaders, advocates and women in all spheres of life to support fellow women and contribute to nation building.

The organisation promotes activities, ideas and projects that advance the welfare and empowerment of women at all levels of society while fostering inclusivity and gender equality.

Membership

Membership consists of women from all walks of life regardless of educational background or career path. ICARE promotes a diverse and inclusive network welcoming professionals, entrepreneurs, community leaders and advocates.

Sister bodies may be created in polytechnics, monotechnics and colleges of education to expand representation.

Objectives

  • Empower and support less privileged women.
  • Support aged women, widows and retirees.
  • Execute programs and projects that benefit women.
  • Promote collaboration and professional development.
  • Encourage women participation in politics and leadership.
  • Advocate for women rights at grassroots and national levels.
  • Promote gender equality and inclusivity.
  • Propose gender-centered policies to government.
  • Support rural women, minorities and physically challenged women.
  • Increase opportunities for women's development.

Rules

  • No spam or promotional messages on platforms.
  • Members must be respectful and courteous.
  • Respect privacy of all members.
  • No hate speech, bullying or religious conflict posts.
  • Encourage open-minded discussion.
  • Members should participate actively.
  • Members must fulfill assigned responsibilities.
  • Ideas for improvement are always welcome.

Our Programs

Women Empowerment

Empowering less privileged women within and outside university communities through mentorship, training, and support programs.

Support for Widows & Elderly Women

Providing assistance, care initiatives, and community support for aged women, widows, and retirees across Nigeria.

Community Development Project

Designing and executing impactful programs, initiatives, and outreach projects that benefit women and communities.

Advocacy & Gender Equality

Promoting gender equality and inclusivity while advocating for policies that improve the welfare of women nationwide.

Skills Development & Mentorship

Providing professional development opportunities, skills training, mentorship, and resources for women's growth.
